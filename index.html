About Me

Hello! I'm Meseret, a dedicated and ambitious Information Technology student at Assosa University in Ethiopia. Technology isn't just what I study—it's my passion and my future.

Growing up in Ethiopia, I witnessed firsthand how technology can transform communities and create opportunities. This inspired me to pursue IT and become part of the digital revolution happening across Africa. At Assosa University, I'm learning not just to code, but to solve real problems that matter to people.

I'm particularly interested in web development, mobile applications, and how we can use technology to improve education, healthcare, and business in our communities. Every day, I challenge myself to learn something new—whether it's a programming language, a design principle, or a new way to think about problems.

Beyond academics, I enjoy collaborating with fellow students on projects, participating in tech workshops, and sharing my knowledge with others who are interested in technology. I believe that the future of Ethiopia is digital, and I want to be part of building that future.

My mission: To become a skilled IT professional who creates innovative solutions that make technology accessible and beneficial for all Ethiopians.
